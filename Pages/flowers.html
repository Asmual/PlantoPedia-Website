Flowers Gallary

Water Lily

A beautiful water plant that symbolizes peace. It blooms mostly during the Monsoon.

Rose

The most popular flower for its beauty and scent. It grows best in Winter.

Camelia

A royal-looking flower with thick petals. It loves the cool Winter breeze.

Joba

A bright and hardy flower with many colors. You can see it blooming all year round.

Bagan Bilash

A very colorful plant that loves the sun. It blooms heavily in Spring and Summer.

Bely

Tiny white flowers that smell amazing. They bloom beautifully during Summer and Monsoon.

Shiuly

The signature flower of Autumn. It blooms at night and has a heavenly smell.

Shondha Maloti

Also called the 'Four O'Clock' flower because it opens in the Late Afternoon.

MariGold

Bright orange and yellow flowers used for festivals. They bloom mainly in Winter.

Dalia

Large, stunning flowers with many patterns. They are the stars of the Winter garden.

Daisy

Simple and cheerful white flowers with yellow centers. They bloom in Spring and Summer.

Rongon

Beautiful clusters of tiny flowers. This hardy plant blooms throughout the Summer.

Indoor Plants

Money Plant

Scientific name: Epipremnum aureum. Thrives in indirect sunlight and needs moderate watering.

Monstera

Scientific name: Monstera deliciosa. Prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

Snake Plant

Scientific name: Dracaena trifasciata. A hardy plant that survives in low light and needs little water.

Aglonima

Scientific name: Aglaonema commutatum. Loves shady spots and humid environments; keep soil moist.

Peace Lily

Scientific name: Spathiphyllum. Needs low to medium light and consistent moisture to bloom beautifully.

Syngonium

Scientific name: Syngonium podophyllum. Prefers bright, indirect light and regular misting to maintain humidity.

Lucky Bamboo

Scientific name: Dracaena sanderiana. Grows best in clean water or moist soil under low light conditions.

Aloe Vera

Scientific name: Aloe barbadensis miller. Loves bright sunlight and requires very minimal watering.

Anthurium

Scientific name: Anthurium andraeanum. Requires bright indirect light and high humidity for its waxy flowers.

Philodendron (burle marx)

Scientific name: Philodendron 'Burle Marx'. A fast-growing plant that loves filtered sun and moist soil.

Daisy

Simple and cheerful white flowers with yellow centers. They bloom in Spring and Summer.

Rongon

Beautiful clusters of tiny flowers. This hardy plant blooms throughout the Summer.
